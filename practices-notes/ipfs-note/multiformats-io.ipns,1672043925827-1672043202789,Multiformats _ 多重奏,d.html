#Self-describing values for Future-proofing面向未来的自描述值

Every choice in computing has a tradeoff. This includes formats, algorithms, encodings, and so on. And even with a great deal of planning, decisions may lead to breaking changes down the road, or to solutions which are no longer optimal. Allowing systems to evolve and grow is important.计算中的每个选择都有一个权衡。这包括格式、算法、编码，等等。即使有大量的计划，决定也可能导致在未来的道路上发生破坏性的变化，或者导致解决方案不再是最佳的。允许系统进化和成长是很重要的。

#What are Multiformats?什么是Multiformats？

The Multiformats Project is a collection of protocols which aim to future-proof systems, today. They do this mainly by enhancing format values with self-description. This allows interoperability, protocol agility, and helps us avoid lock in.Multiformats项目是一个协议的集合，旨在为今天的系统提供未来。他们主要是通过加强格式值的自我描述来实现这一目标。这允许互操作性、协议敏捷性，并帮助我们避免锁定。

The self-describing aspects of the protocols have a few stipulations:协议的自我描述方面有一些规定。

  • They MUST be in-band (with the value); not out-of-band (in context).它们必须是带内的（与值一起）；而不是带外的（在环境中）。
  • They MUST avoid lock-in and promote extensibility.它们必须避免锁定并促进可扩展性。
  • They MUST be compact and have a binary-packed representation.它们必须是紧凑的，并且有一个二进制包装的表示。
  • They MUST have a human-readable representation.它们必须有一个人类可读的表示。

#Multiformat protocols多格式协议

Currently, the following multiformat protocols exist:目前，存在以下多格式协议。

  • multihash - self-describing hashesmultihash - 自我描述的哈希值
  • multiaddr (WIP) - self-describing network addressesmultiaddr（WIP）--自我描述的网络地址
  • multibase (WIP) - self-describing base encodingsmultibase（WIP）--自我描述的基础编码
  • multicodec - self-describing serializationmulticodec - 自我描述的序列化
  • multistream (DEPRECATED) - self-describing stream network protocolsmultistream (DEPRECATED) - 自我描述的流网络协议
  • multigram (WIP) - self-describing packet network protocolsmultigram (WIP) - 自我描述的数据包网络协议

Each of the projects has its list of implementations in various languages.每个项目都有其在各种语言中的实现列表。

#Project Status项目状态

Several of the multiformats have stable specs and stable implementations. We’re are working on the others. We prioritize their usage as soon as possible, as what they offer – protocol interoperability and future-proofing – has real-world consequences today.几个多格式项目都有稳定的规格和稳定的实现。我们正在研究其他的。我们优先考虑尽快使用它们，因为它们所提供的东西--协议的互操作性和面向未来--在今天具有现实世界的影响。

Towards that end, we are encouraging improvements to WIP protocols, and implementations of all. Please contribute to the projects on GitHub.为此，我们鼓励对WIP协议的改进，以及对所有协议的实现。请为GitHub上的项目作出贡献。

#A note on the word Multiformats关于Multiformats这个词的说明

Multiformats is the name for the organization, but it can also be used to refer to protocols; for instance, in the sentence “Use one of the multiformats”. Formats is interchangeable with protocols, here. We try to capitalize Multiformats when it refers to the organization, on GitHub.Multiformats是该组织的名称，但它也可以用来指代协议；例如，在 "使用多种格式中的一种 "这句话中。Formats在这里可以与协议互换。在GitHub上，当Multiformats指的是该组织时，我们尽量将其大写。
