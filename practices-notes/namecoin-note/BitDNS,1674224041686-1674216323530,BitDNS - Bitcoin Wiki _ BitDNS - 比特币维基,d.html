Bitdns was project with the goal to extend Bitcoin's technology to a domain name service, expanding the software to support transactions for registering, updating, and transferring domains. The project eventually became an altchain with its own altcoin, known as Namecoin. Bitdns 是一个旨在将比特币技术扩展到域名服务的项目，扩展该软件以支持注册、更新和转移域的交易。该项目最终成为一个具有自己的山寨币的山寨链，称为 Namecoin。
