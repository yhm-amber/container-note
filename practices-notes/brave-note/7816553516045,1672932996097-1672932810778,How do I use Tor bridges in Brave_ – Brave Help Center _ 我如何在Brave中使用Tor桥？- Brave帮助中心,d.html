How do I use Tor bridges in Brave? Follow如何在 Brave 中使用 Tor 桥接？

Tor bridgesTor桥接

Most pluggable transports, such as obfs4, rely on the use of "bridge" relays. Like ordinary Tor relays, bridges are run by volunteers; unlike ordinary relays, however, they are not listed publicly, so an adversary cannot identify them easily.大多数可插拔的传输系统，如obfs4，都依赖于 "桥接 "中继的使用。与普通的Tor中继一样，桥接是由志愿者运行的；然而，与普通的中继不同，它们没有被公开列出，所以对手不能轻易识别它们。

Using bridges in combination with pluggable transports helps to conceal the fact that you are using Tor, but may slow down the connection compared to using ordinary Tor relays.结合使用桥接器和可插拔传输器有助于掩盖你正在使用Tor的事实，但与使用普通Tor中继相比，可能会降低连接速度。

Other pluggable transports, like meek, use different anti-censorship techniques that do not rely on bridges. You do not need to obtain bridge addresses in order to use these transports.其他可插拔传输器，如meek，使用不同的反审查技术，不依赖桥接。你不需要获得桥接地址来使用这些传输。

Getting bridge addresses获取网桥地址

Because bridge addresses are not public, you will need to request them yourself. You have a few options:因为桥接地址是不公开的，你需要自己去申请。你有几个选择。

  • Visit https://bridges.torproject.org/options and follow the instructions, or访问 https://bridges.torproject.org/options 并按照说明操作，或
  • Email bridges@torproject.org from a Gmail, or Riseup email address从 Gmail 或 Riseup 的电子邮件地址发送电子邮件到 bridges@torproject.org
  • Use Moat to fetch bridges from within Brave.使用Moat从Brave内部获取桥梁。
  • Send a message to @GetBridgesBot on Telegram. Tap on Start or write /start or /bridges in the chat.在Telegram上给@GetBridgesBot发送消息。点击开始或在聊天中写下/开始或/桥梁。

Using Moat使用Moat

From Brave, click on Settings in the hamburger menu (≡) and then on "Privacy and security" in the sidebar. Look for Tor connection settings and click on it. Toggle the  Use a bridge,  option "on", then select Request a bridge from torproject.org, click Request a New Bridge... for BridgeDB to provide a bridge. Complete the Captcha and click Submit.在Brave中，点击汉堡包菜单（≡）中的设置，然后点击侧边栏中的 "隐私和安全"。寻找Tor连接设置并点击它。切换使用桥接，选项 "on"，然后选择Request a bridge from torproject.org，点击Request a New Bridge... for BridgeDB to provide a bridge。完成验证码并点击提交。

Entering bridge addresses输入桥接地址

From Brave, click on Main Menu --> Settings and then on Privacy and security in the sidebar. Look for Tor connection settings and click on it. Select the checkbox Use a bridge,  and from the option Provide a bridge, enter each bridge address on a separate line.在Brave，点击主菜单-->设置，然后点击侧边栏的隐私和安全。寻找Tor连接设置并点击它。选择复选框 "使用网桥"，并从 "提供网桥 "选项中，在单独一行输入每个网桥地址。

If the connection fails, the bridges you received may be down. Please use one of the above methods to obtain more bridge addresses, and try again.如果连接失败，你收到的网桥可能已经失效。请使用上述方法之一获得更多的网桥地址，并再次尝试。
