[site]: https://openrgb.org/ "One of the biggest complaints about RGB is the software ecosystem surrounding it. Every manufacturer has their own app, their own brand, their own style. If you want to mix and match devices, you end up with a ton of conflicting, functionally identical apps competing for your background resources. On top of that, these apps are proprietary and Windows-only. Some even require online accounts. What if there was a way to control all of your RGB devices from a single app, on both Windows and Linux, without any nonsense? That is what OpenRGB sets out to achieve. One app to rule them all. // 对 RGB 最大的抱怨之一是它周围的软件生态系统。每个制造商都有自己的应用程序、自己的品牌、自己的风格。如果您想混合和匹配设备，最终会出现大量相互冲突、功能相同的应用程序争夺您的后台资源。最重要的是，这些应用程序是专有的且仅限 Windows。有些甚至需要在线帐户。如果有一种方法可以在 Windows 和 Linux 上通过单个应用程序控制所有 RGB 设备，而无需任何废话，那会怎么样？这就是 OpenRGB 想要实现的目标。一款应用程序即可统治一切。"
