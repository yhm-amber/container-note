How to get namecoins如何获得域名币

Namecoins are a token-like currency used to register and update Namecoin names. If you want to register a Namecoin name, you’ll need to obtain some namecoins.Namecoins 是一种类似令牌的货币，用于注册和更新 Namecoin 名称。如果您想注册一个域名币名称，您需要获得一些域名币。

Prerequisites先决条件

To get namecoins, you first need a Namecoin wallet.要获得域名币，您首先需要一个域名币钱包。

Exchanges交易所

The easiest way to get namecoins is to purchase them on an exchange. See the list of exchanges.获得名称币的最简单方法是在交易所购买。查看交易所列表。

You can check current Namecoin prices at CryptoCoinCharts, current Namecoin prices at Bisq, and Namecoin historical price charts at BitcoinWisdom.您可以在 CryptoCoinCharts 查看当前的 Namecoin 价格，在 Bisq 查看当前的 Namecoin 价格，以及在 BitcoinWisdom 的 Namecoin 历史价格图表。

Mining矿业

If you don’t wish to purchase namecoins on an exchange, you can also mine them.如果您不想在交易所购买名称币，您也可以开采它们。

Namecoins can be mined in the same way as bitcoins. However, it is recommended to do merged mining, i.e. namecoins are mined as a free by-product of Bitcoin mining.Namecoins 可以像比特币一样被开采。但是，建议进行联合挖矿，即名称币作为比特币挖矿的免费副产品进行开采。

For a list of current Namecoin mining pools, see our Metrics data. Ask your favorite Bitcoin mining pool to offer merged mining of Namecoin in case they don’t.有关当前 Namecoin 矿池的列表，请参阅我们的指标数据。要求你最喜欢的比特币矿池提供 Namecoin 的联合挖矿，以防他们不这样做。

You can also run your own mining pool. Eloipool and P2Pool software has support for merged mining.您也可以运行自己的矿池。 Eloipool和P2Pool软件支持联合挖矿。

If you don’t have specialized hardware (ASICs) for Bitcoin mining, consider mining a CPU-friendly or GPU-friendly coin and then exchanging it for NMC (or for BTC and then NMC).如果您没有用于比特币挖掘的专用硬件 (ASIC)，请考虑挖掘 CPU 友好型或 GPU 友好型硬币，然后将其交换为 NMC（或 BTC，然后再交换 NMC）。
